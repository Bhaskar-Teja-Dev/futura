02. Description of service

Digital Rebel / Futura is a web application oriented toward long-horizon savings and retirement planning for adults (including Gen Z–aged users). Core capabilities include: sign-in with Google, onboarding and goal capture, dashboards, manual contribution logging, educational “Finance Bite” style content, and projection tooling.

Explicitly out of scope for v1 (per product spec): direct broker integrations; Open Banking / PSD2 sync; regulated personal financial advice; native mobile apps; social comparison features; employer pension sync; multi-currency trading engines; and automated portfolio rebalancing. Availability of any feature on the live site is subject to what we actually ship.

We do not operate a traditional password store for Google sign-in: session and token handling follow Supabase and your provider’s rules. Row Level Security (RLS) in our database layer is designed so user-scoped data is only accessible under your authenticated identity.

04. Projections & no advice

Projections are illustrative only. They are not live market quotes, promises, or personal financial advice.

Example engines may use long-run global equities assumptions (for instance ~7% annual growth informed by broad index history such as MSCI World over multi-decade samples). Actual returns vary; you may lose principal. Markets are volatile.

Digital Rebel is a planning and education tool, not a bank, broker, or regulated adviser in any jurisdiction. Consult a qualified professional before making investment or tax decisions.
