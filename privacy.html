Privacy

This page summarizes how Digital Rebel handles information when you use our product. It is provided for transparency and does not replace professional legal advice.

Account & sign-in

If you sign in with Google or another provider, we receive identifiers and profile details that the provider shares with us according to your consent with that provider. We use this to create and secure your account.

Product data

Data you enter in the app (for example goals, projections, or preferences) is processed to power features you request. Treat all numeric displays as illustrative unless we explicitly label them as live or sourced from a regulated feed.

Cookies & storage

We use browser storage to keep you signed in and to remember UI preferences. You can clear site data in your browser at any time; you may need to sign in again.

Contact

For privacy questions, use Support and we will route your request appropriately.
